'DWTS' Troupe Member Alexis Warr Awaits to Be a Pro for Season 32

Posted by Kelle Repass on Sunday, August 11, 2024

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent Recap

CR: Ray Mickshaw/FOX

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Dancing With The Stars troupe member Alexis Warr has expressed her desire to take a pro role in the show’s upcoming season. Amid uncertainties, fans are also hopeful for the talented ballroom dancer.

Alexis Warr Hopes to be Promoted to DWTS Pro Next Season

During an interview with The U.S. Sun, Warr claimed that she’s ready to take on the DWTS pro if given the chance. Furthermore, the troupe member stated that the show loves to keep them on their toes.

“Right now, I’m just kind of doing what I can to prepare for the possibility of becoming a pro and just working on chasing my dreams and doing dance jobs here and there. And yeah, just trying to keep my brain and body occupied before, you know, that big announcement,” she said.

Season 32 of Dancing With The Stars will begin in the fall this year. Apparently, a lot of pros have welcomed babies in 2022 and 2023. Thus, fans expect that some mamas wouldn’t be able to compete on the show. Nonetheless, Sharna Burgess, Peta Murgatroyd, Daniella Karagach, and Jenna Johnson expressed that they still want to join the pro cast in the upcoming season.

Be that as it may, Witney Carson and Lindsay Arnold will seemingly take some time off. Furthermore, since Cheryl Burke has retired from the show, there may really be an open spot for Warr next season.

Fans Agree that She Should Take a Pro Role on the Show

For those who may not know, Warr won So You Think You Can Dance season 17. She had an idea of what happens in the life of a DWTS pro when she replaced Karagach during a week of season 31. Now though, Warr appears to be determined and prepared to be promoted as pro.

Interestingly, fans agree that Warr should be a pro for season 32. On Reddit, users talk about how she would make a great addition to the pro lineup.

“Oh, that pro spot is coming. I have no doubt of it,” one user wrote. “Her star is rising rapidly and I just know she’ll shine even brighter this year.”

Meanwhile, some users also believe that another troupe member Ezra Sosa should be promoted.

“I hope her & Ezra get promoted to pros she did well last season and Ezra I think is due a pro slot since he’s been on the side the past 2 Seasons,” a person wrote.

While several fan conversations have been ongoing, Dancing With The Stars hasn’t released specific details about season 32 yet.
