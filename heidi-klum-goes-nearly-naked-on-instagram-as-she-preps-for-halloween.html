America’s Got Talent judge Heidi Klum posted a pretty risqué photo on Instagram on Sunday as she went nearly naked to show off her pre-Halloween tan. The model always goes all out for the holiday, and it looks like this year will be no different.

Heidi Klum Goes Nearly Naked on Instagram

In the photo Klum posted, she’s wearing only a skimpy thong, which she pulls down to show her tan lines. One of her breasts is covered by her arm, while the other one is covered by a flash of light. The model captioned the photo, “Halloween Prep.”

She added that the first step of the process for getting ready for Halloween was tanning with Jimmy Coco, the co-founder of Jimmy Coco Sunless Cosmetics. Klum has comments disabled on the post, but we’re sure people would have plenty to say about the revealing shot if they could.

Klum is clearly comfortable stripping down, as she’s famous for being a Victoria’s Secret model. More recently, she posed in a lingerie campaign for Kim Kardashian’s brand SKIMS, and she also posed with her daughter Leni in a recent underwear shoot.

Klum Is Preparing for Halloween 2022

Klum is known for her elaborate Halloween costumes, some of which take hours of preparation. The fact that Klum got a tan might give us a clue about what she’ll be this year, although it’s not entirely clear yet.

The model also took to Instagram on Monday to tease the process of getting ready. She showed off tables full of supplies, captioning the video, “a little color, a little paint, makes you what you ain’t.” Klum also went live on Amazon, where she appeared to be getting prosthetics put on her face.

Klum’s past costumes have included Jessica Rabbit, Princess Fiona from Shrek, an old woman, and an alien. We can’t wait to find out what she has up her sleeve this year.
