If you take a look at your check leaf, you will see a 9-digit number that appears in the middle and printed on the MICR of your financial document. However, sometimes you don’t have a check leaf in your hands, but you need to find out First Bank sort code in Lagos or other areas in Nigeria. How can you manage this task? Where should you look for First Bank general sort code or branch’s codes?

In this post, we would like to talk about the sort code for First Bank of Nigeria. We will cover all the branches. Thus, you will always have a reliable source of the bank’s codes, when you need them.

What is First Bank sort code?

Different branches of the same financial organisation have different sort codes. Knowing the First Bank sort code, for example, allows transferring your funds between the branches of the same bank or between two banks.
