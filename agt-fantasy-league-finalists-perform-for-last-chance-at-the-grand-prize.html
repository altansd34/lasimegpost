On Monday night, AGT: Fantasy League aired the first part of the Finals, as the Top 10 acts took the stage for their last chance at being named the winner and earning the $250,000 grand prize. The results will be announced next week.

AGT: Fantasy League Finalists Take the Stage

During Monday’s show, Simon Cowell and Howie Mandel each had three acts competing on their dream teams. Cowell’s team included Pack Drumline, Musa Motha, and Sainted, who sang a powerful rendition of “Like a Prayer” by Madonna.

The performance earned a standing ovation from the judges. Cowell told the choir that he felt like he was “literally in heaven.” Mel B told them, “You can sing anything and everything.” She added that she “felt it in my belly, in my soul.”

The finalists on Mandel’s team included Kodi Lee, the Ramadhani Brothers, and Shadow Ace, who invited judge Heidi Klum on stage for some fun with shadow puppets. He even featured Klum’s song “Chai Tea with Heidi.” Shadow Ace is one of only two acts in the Finals (along with Pack Drumline) who were voted through by the audience rather than a Golden Buzzer from a judge.

Who Else Competed in the Finals?

Heidi Klum and Mel B both had two acts on each of their teams in the Finals. For Klum, those acts were Sofie Dossi and V. Unbeatable, who showed off a spectacular acrobatic dance routine that had the crowd going wild.

“No one does it like you guys do it,” Klum told the group after their performance, which earned a standing ovation from the panel. “No one jumps as high as you all do, No one wants it as much as you do.”

Meanwhile, Mel B’s team featured Billy & Emily England and Aidan Bryant, who showed off an aerial routine unlike anything we’ve seen from him before. After the performance, Mel said Cowell told her it was “annoying” how good it was.

Tune in next Monday night at 8/7c on NBC to find out which of the finalists earned enough votes to be named the winner of AGT: Fantasy League. Who are you rooting for? Let us know in the comments below.
