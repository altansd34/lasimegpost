The Black Swan is one of the fan favorites this season on The Masked Singer, even more so now that she created a hilariously awkward moment on stage with Nick Cannon. In a new clue to her identity, The Black Swan revealed that she has Cannon’s ex-wife Mariah Carey on her mind which took him by surprise.

The Black Swan Makes Shocking Revelation About Nick Cannon’s Ex-Wife Mariah Carey

During tonight’s episode, Cannon introduced The Masked Singer Mind Reader. This contraption reveals what is on each of the contestant’s minds. This will help the judges get deeper clues about which celebrities are under the masks.

After her performance, it was time for The Black Swan to step behind the mind reader machine. It revealed that she has been thinking about Carey.

“This is awkward,” Nicole Scherzinger said after the big clue reveal.

Cannon looked completely shocked when he saw Carey’s face pop up on the screen.

“That’s exactly what’s on my mind as well,” Cannon confessed. “There’s a few others with her, but she in there.”

It’s All Love Between Cannon And Carey Even After Their Divorce

The 40-year-old took it like a champ and still showed his love for Carey. The pair share 10-year-old twins Monroe and Moroccan. Their divorce was settled in 2016 but they have still remained dedicated to co-parenting their children.

“For the record I’m a huge fan of hers,” The Black Swan says about Carey to which Cannon replied “me too.”

The Black Swan’s connection to Carey is definitely music related.

 “Surpassing her record one time was just one of the biggest moments of my entire life” the masked songstress revealed.

Based on the clues, *spoiler alert* we think The Black Swan is singer JoJo.

“I beat her at Connect Four one time” Cannon joked which made all of The Masked Singer judges laugh.
