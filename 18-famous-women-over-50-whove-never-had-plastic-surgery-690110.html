18 Famous Women Over 50 Whove Never Had Plastic Surgery

Posted by Tandra Barner on Friday, August 23, 2024

We’re quite used to celebs often undergoing plastic surgery to look younger. “New” noses and plumped up lips don’t even impress us anymore. But even among celebrities, there are women who aren’t afraid of aging and say “no” to botox and to going under the knife.

Bright Side is super-impressed with the stories of some popular models and actresses who age gracefully. In this article, you’ll see photos of 18 beauties taken at different times and they still look the same!

18. Isabella Rossellini

In one of her interviews, the actress said that she would never countenance cosmetic surgery. Here’s Isabella’s recipe for youth: a mix of organic foods and good facial cream.

17. Sharon Stone

Sharon Stone works out 4-5 times a week and buys drugstore face creams. In an interview, the actress claimed that she’d never had plastic surgery.

16. Brooke Shields

The model admits that she tried Botox but didn’t like the result. But Brooke doesn’t want to turn to a surgeon: she’s scared that she’ll end up looking like the Joker.

15. Salma Hayek

In different interviews, the actress has said that she’s never gotten plastic surgery.

14. Jodie Foster

Jodie Foster thinks that modern society makes women transform themselves according to fashion standards. The actress is against plastic surgery and she isn’t afraid of wrinkles.

13. Andie MacDowell

Andie MacDowell is sure that she needs neither botox nor a facelift. She simply takes care of her skin and leads a healthy lifestyle.

12. Emma Thompson

The actress, together with Kate Winslet and Rachel Weisz, has founded the British Anti-Cosmetic Surgery League. Emma dubs Botox injections “mad.” She doesn’t recommend them to anyone.

11. Sigourney Weaver

Recently, the actress has said that she liked aging and there was nothing awful about wrinkles.

10. Lauren Hutton

The ’70s top model is proud of her wrinkles. Lauren looks magnificent thanks to coconut oil, which she’s been using since she was 22.

9. Halle Berry

The actress claims that she’s never gotten plastic surgery. Halle Berry is a sports fan: on her Instagram, she shares pictures of her workouts.

8. Cindy Crawford

The top model takes care of her skin and works out regularly. Cindy likes microdermabrasion masks, sunscreen, and moisturizing products.

7. Julianne Moore

Julianne Moore claims she doesn’t care about aging. The actress does yoga and loves cardio workouts.

6. Meryl Streep

Meryl Streep always says that she’s against plastic surgery. Her look proves that women are like fine wine: they only get better with age.

5. Sissy Spacek

Sissy Spacek has never turned to plastic surgery but says she’s not against photo retouching.

4. Sophie Marceau

This French actress has never drastically changed her looks. And the way she looked at the Annual César Film Awards only proves that she still can impress her fans.

3. Isabelle Huppert

Isabelle Huppert is aging gracefully. Her secret to looking great is a combination of high-quality makeup and elegant outerwear.

2. Juliette Binoche

This French actress often sneers at women’s love for Botox and breast implants. She only recommends drinking more water to look great.

1. Victoria Ruffo

The Mexican actress and star of Simply Maria the TV series loves natural beauty. On her Instagram, she posts her no-filter photos without any regret.

Well, we’re impressed with these women’s courage! They’re not afraid of aging and don’t care about wrinkles. Who inspires you the most?

Please note: This article was updated in April 2022 to correct source material and factual inaccuracies.
