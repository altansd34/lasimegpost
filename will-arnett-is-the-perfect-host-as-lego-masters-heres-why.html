Will Arnett is Perfect as The Host of 'LEGO Masters' Heres Why

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, August 31, 2024

CR: Tom Griscom/FOX

Reading Time: 2 minutes

In the world of reality television, there are a few key ingredients that make a show successful: an engaging concept, talented contestants, and a charismatic host. When it comes to Fox’s hit competition series LEGO Masters, all of these elements are in place, but it’s the show’s host, Will Arnett, who truly brings it all together.

What Makes Will Arnett Such a Great Host on LEGO Masters

Known for his comedic roles in Arrested Development and BoJack Horseman, Will Arnett the perfect choice to guide viewers through the world of LEGO building. He’s a self-proclaimed LEGO enthusiast, and his enthusiasm for the show is infectious. He’s also a quick wit with a knack for making the contestants and judges laugh, which helps to keep the show light and entertaining.

Beyond his comedic chops, Arnett also has a genuine appreciation for the creativity and artistry that goes into LEGO building. He’s able to connect with the contestants on a personal level, and he’s not afraid to get down on their level and help them out when they need it.

His passion for LEGO is evident from the moment he steps on screen. He’s able to talk about the bricks with a level of knowledge and enthusiasm that’s contagious. Arnett is a master of improv and delivery. He knows how to make the contestants and judges laugh without ever crossing the line into mean-spiritedness.

He is also genuinely interested in the contestants’ stories and their passion for LEGO. Arnett is able to make them feel comfortable and confident, which helps them to bring their best work to the table.

Will Arnett is an Active Participant in LEGO Masters

In addition to these qualities, Arnett also brings a unique perspective to the show as a host. He’s not just there to read cue cards and introduce the judges; he’s an active participant in the competition. He’s often seen helping the contestants with their builds, and he’s always willing to offer advice and encouragement.

Arnett’s presence on the show has been instrumental in its success. He’s certainly able to make LEGO Masters a family-friendly show that’s both entertaining and inspiring. He’s also a true champion of creativity and imagination, and he’s a role model for aspiring LEGO builders everywhere.

Arnett has served as the host of LEGO Masters for the show’s entire run, now on its fourth season on Fox. His leadership and enthusiasm have provided an entertaining through-line for contestants and viewers across over 40 episodes of brick-building creativity and fun.
