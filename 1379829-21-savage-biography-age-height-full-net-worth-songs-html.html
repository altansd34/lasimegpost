21 Savage biography: age, height, full name, net worth, songs

Posted by Kelle Repass on Sunday, August 4, 2024

21 Savage is a hip-hop rapper, songwriter, and record producer. The artist, who was born in the United Kingdom, moved to the United States of America when he was 7. Today, he is one of the biggest names in the hip-hop industry.

The singer is popular for mixtapes such as The Slaughter Tape and Slaughter King. He has released hits such as Bank Account, Can't Leave Without You, Monster, Secret, and Yessirkskii.

21 Savage deported

On 3rd February 2019, the talented rapper was arrested by the US Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE). The ICE officials reported that he was a UK citizen who entered the United States in July 2005 and unlawfully overstayed even upon the expiry of his visa in July 2016.

On 12th February, he was granted bond and released the following day awaiting the outcome of an expedited deportation hearing. Although the hearing was scheduled to take place on 9th April, it was postponed indefinitely.
