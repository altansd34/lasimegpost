'AGT All-Stars' Finalist Tom Ball Releases Soulful New Single 'Falling Slowly'

Posted by Billy Koelling on Thursday, August 8, 2024

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent America's Got Talent: All-Stars

Photo by: Trae Patton/NBC

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Tom Ball, the talented singer who placed third on season 15 of Britain’s Got Talent, just released a new song. The track, “Falling Slowly” is a cover of the beautiful song from Once the musical, showcases Ball’s soulful voice and emotive style.

Tom Ball Drops New Single ‘Falling Slowly’

Ball’s rendition of “Falling Slowly” showcases his ability to convey deep emotions through his singing. His soulful voice and heartfelt interpretation of the song’s lyrics are sure to touch the hearts of listeners.

In an Instagram post, Ball expressed his excitement about the release of “Falling Slowly.” The song is now available on all streaming platforms and can be downloaded for free via his mailing list.

Ball has been captivating audiences with his incredible voice since he first gained fame on Britain’s Got Talent. He then took his talents to the AGT All-Stars, stage where he earned a Golden Buzzer from all the judges and host Terry Crews.

This latest release is just one of many impressive performances from Ball. He has previously wowed audiences with his covers of Sam Smith’s “Lay Me Down,” and “Writing’s on the Wall,” and his ability to convey emotions through his singing has earned him a devoted following.

His Got Talent Journey

After finishing third on BGT, he went on to expand his music teaching skills by creating an online music school, Tom Ball’s Rising Stars. This music school provides opportunities for individuals to develop their musical skills.

However, his passion for singing didn’t stop there. In late January, he surprised viewers with his impressive rendition of Simon and Garfunkel’s “The Sound of Silence,” on AGT All-Stars. This performance earned him a group Golden Buzzer and high praise from judge Simon Cowell.

Fans of the show were shocked to discover the truth about his past, including his history of performing at pubs. In addition, his early love of singing that started with Les Miserables songs. Despite his journey from small pubs to the world’s biggest stages, Ball’s dedication to music education and his own passion for singing has never wavered.
