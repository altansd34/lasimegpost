A Granny Was Called Mad for Getting Her 1st Tattoo at 91, Yet the Ink Art Is Just as Lovely as

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, August 29, 2024

At the age of 91, a grandmother surprised us all by getting a tattoo. Yet, beneath the surface, her decision held a deeper meaning. The chosen tattoo became a poignant symbol of something way bigger than we can think of.

A grandma decided to have her first tattoo at 91.

Guada Kelly’s lovely great-grandkids hopped on TikTok to spread some joy by sharing an adorable video of their grandma. The video has been a huge hit, racking up an impressive 4.3 million views and counting.

In a curious twist, Guada’s four amazing great-grandchildren recently decided to have a little tattoo party, and guess who decided to join in on the fun? That’s right — the spirited 91-year-old Granny Guada herself. With youthful enthusiasm, she got her very first tattoo, making the day even more special and memorable.

The tattoo clearly divided the Internet.

While Guada’s grandchildren were thrilled about the wonderful bonding experience, it’s worth noting that not everyone among the nearly four million viewers who enjoyed her story shared the same enthusiasm for her decision to get a tattoo at her age.

One person commented, “Weird and sad.” Another individual found her actions a bit “ridiculous,” while several other comments conveyed disapproval saying she is “mad”.

Opinions were quite diverse regarding the tattoo — some found it unappealing, while others playfully recommended including her name and phone number, jesting that it’s perhaps wiser to have a lifelong mark at 91 than at 24.

A portion of social media users even went as far as labeling Guada’s choice as imprudent and irresponsible. However, amid the array of viewpoints, it’s worth highlighting that Guada and her great-grandchildren also garnered a collection of heartwarming and encouraging comments.

One person marveled at Guada, calling her “amazing,” while another hailed the shared experience as “awesome.” The video also received an outpouring of affection. In the video posted by her grandchildren, Guada sits comfortably in the tattoo studio as the artist positions a stencil on her upper arm. She looks down at her arm, smiling at the stencil, and gives two thumbs up after the artist takes off the stencil paper.

She looks so happy.

The exact design remains a mystery, but her great-grandchildren also get matching stencils. Despite the potential pain, Guada smiles and jokes with her great-grandchildren as the artist works on her tiny flower tattoo above her elbow. When the tattoo is finished, she admires the artwork—a lasting reminder of the special shared experience with her grandchildren.

Her grandkids got matching tattoos.

Guada’s special bond with her great-grandchildren was beautifully sealed with matching flower tattoos, each etched onto distinct parts of their bodies. Guada proudly wore hers on her arm while her great-grandson showcased his on his calf. The choice of placement for her great-granddaughters was equally unique, with the delicate flowers gracing their ribs and arms.

Despite being in her early 90s, Guada’s bright spirit shines through in her videos. She effortlessly reads and sings along to song lyrics with infectious enthusiasm, radiating vibrant and joyful energy.

Guada’s presence on the internet has ignited a sensation, captivating viewers worldwide. Her videos are receiving an outpouring of appreciation, being liked, shared, and flooded with comments from newfound admirers who are enthusiastically spreading their love and admiration for her across the globe.

She doesn’t let mean comments bother her. Instead, she sees them as ways to get better. With a strong and positive attitude, she keeps moving forward and doesn’t let anything bring her down.

A tattoo is a lifelong mark that tells your unique story, a canvas of memories and beliefs etched onto your skin. It’s a powerful way to express your identity, passions, and milestones, carrying their significance with you wherever you go. Each inked design becomes a permanent reminder of your personal journey and the moments that shape who you are.
