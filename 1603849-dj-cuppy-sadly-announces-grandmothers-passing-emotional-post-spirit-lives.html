DJ Cuppy Sadly Announces Grandmother's Passing in Emotional Post, Fans React: &quot;Your Spirit Lives On&quot;

Posted by Billy Koelling on Sunday, August 25, 2024
  • Florence Otedola, aka DJ Cuppy, has just shared an emotional post about the death of her maternal grandmother
  • The disc jockey took to her social media pages to share a heartfelt farewell message to Grandma Kaduna
  • The billionaire heiress' post stirred many emotional reactions online from celebs and her fan base

Nigerian billionaire heiress DJ Cuppy buzzed social media after she shared a post about the passing of her maternal grandmother.

She shared a heartfelt post on Twitter, thanking God for a life well lived and appreciating her late grandmother for shaping her mother into who she is today.

Grieving the loss of her grandmother, the master's degree holder shared memories she had with her on her Instagram story. DJ Cuppy posted a picture with Grandma Kaduna and another one of her mother and grandmother.
