Zayn Malik is Marking His New Era with Original Single 'Love Like This'

Posted by Kelle Repass on Monday, August 26, 2024

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

WireImage

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Zayn Malik has finally revealed the title and release date of his new music. Titled “Love Like This,” the former One Direction member announced that the single will be out by the end of the month.

Zayn Malik Officially Announces New Single ‘Love Like This’

After a week of teasing his comeback track on Instagram, the pop star has revealed that his new era is coming real soon. Set for release on July 21, the highly anticipated song will serve as Malik’s first single since 2021.

The “Pillowtalk” hitmaker broke the news through a teaser video posted on Instagram. In the clip, a shirtless Malik is spray-painting the track’s title onto a wall along with the date “7.21.” A snippet of the song is also playing while the pop singer is writing text.

“I guess there’s love like this/ Usually I never wanna jump like this,” Malik sings. “I guess there’s faith like this/ Give you everything and you can skate like this/ But I think I gotta take that risk ’cause I can not go back/ And I’m loving what you say, what you say, what you say when you’re on my line.”

By the glimpse of it, “Love Like This” seems to be a chill yet upbeat track. Such tune elevates its love-infused lyrics, complemented by Malik’s laid-back and effortlessly good musicality. 

Fans Wonder About the Mystery Girl in the Teaser

Last July 7, Malik posted two black-and-white snaps on Instagram, slightly revealing a blonde girl for his upcoming song release. This has quickly made fans intensely curious about the mystery girl’s identity. Since the pop star’s upload, his supporters began speculating who might be the blonde woman behind his new music.

Some fans alleged that the girl could be pop icon Selena Gomez, who has recently replaced her brown locks for blonde hair. Relating Malik’s photo to past rumors, fans think that Gomez has something to do with “Love Like This.” However, the assumption might have been negative as the “Who Says” performer stated on TikTok that she’s currently single.

On the other hand, some users theorized that Malik is “soft-launching” his new lover. Meanwhile, others just think that the blonde girl is just the singer’s love interest for his forthcoming music video.

While speculations continue to arise, fans wouldn’t have to wait for too long as “Love Like This” will be dropping soon.
