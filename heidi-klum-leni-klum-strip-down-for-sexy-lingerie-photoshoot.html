Heidi Klum, Leni Klum Strip Down for Sexy Lingerie Photoshoot

Posted by Patria Henriques on Thursday, August 15, 2024

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent America's Got Talent

Photo by Gotham/FilmMagic

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Mother-daughter duo Heidi and Leni Klum are back at it again. They went to another level with their recent photoshoot for lingerie brand, Intimissimi. The pair showcased their above average beauty and figures in the playful photoshoot celebrating women and the art of Italian lingerie.

Heidi Klum Teams Up With Daughter To Show That Modeling Runs in the Family

Italian lingerie brand Intimissimi launched a new campaign featuring Klum and her 18-year-old daughter as models. The campaign featured the two wearing matching black-and-white lace intimates while posing in a clip of the photoshoot.

The campaign video shows how much they enjoyed their time in front of the camera. They both danced and lip-synced to the music while wearing the brand’s signature undergarments. The video and pictures from the shoot are posted in each of their own Instagram accounts. Their captions make it clear that they thoroughly enjoyed posing together once again.

The America’s Got Talent judge proudly supports her daughter who has seemingly inherited her modeling looks. After the decision of her daughter to go to college, she would continue supporting her in becoming the next-generation supermodel. While Klum is no slouch herself, with looks not normally seen on a 49-year-old woman, her figure is still to die for.

Klum Leaves Out Sofia Vergara on Post About AGT Spinoff

The upcoming spinoff AGT All-Stars announced the judges who will be appearing on the show. The returning host will be Terry Crews, while the confirmed judges are Simon Cowell, Howie Mandel, and Klum herself.

In Klum’s latest post about AGT All-Stars, she posted a picture of herself sitting between Cowell and Mandel. Although Vergara has been confirmed not to be returning, not even a mention of her can be seen in the post.

Fans looked at the post as the model snubbing her fellow judge, sparking rumors of a feud between the two. Adding fuel to the fire is the fact that Vergara has not liked Klum’s post and the comments being disabled.

The fans are nevertheless shocked to learn that Vergara will not be joining the AGT All-Stars cast. There’s been no word as of yet if Vergara will continue to be a judge on the regular series or if they are looking for her replacement.
