Dancing with the Stars pro dancers Danielle Karagach and Pasha Pashkov have shared photos from their maternity shoot. The couple is currently expecting their first child together, which will be a baby girl. The new photos show off Karagach’s baby bump.

Daniella Karagach, Pasha Pashkov Share Maternity Shoot

A few days ago, Karagach shared a set of photos from the couple’s maternity shoot on Instagram. The first shot shows Karagach posing topless with a bouquet of flowers covering her, as Pashkov stands behind her touching her belly.

Other shots show the couple smiling and embracing, as Karagach shows off her baby bump in a lace robe and knitted dress. At the time, Karagach teased that there would be “more to share from this amazing shoot.” She added in the hashtags that she was 32 weeks pregnant.

Karagach kept her promise to share more, posting a set of three photos earlier this week. They show Karagach standing beside a window wearing only a long skirt. She covers her breasts with one hand and touches her belly with the other.

“It’s pretty surreal what our bodies can do,” Karagach wrote in the caption. “I’m soaking up every minute I can with my baby girl inside. Life is beautiful and I’m grateful for this journey. I can’t wait to be your mama little one.”

Karagach and Pashkov’s DWTS friends shared their thoughts on the new photos in the comments. Jenna Johnson called Karagach “the most beautiful mama to be,” while Sharna Burgess and Jessie James Decker both called her “stunning.”

Emma Slater wrote, “You guys I love these shots!!!!” Peta Murgatroyd commented, “Daniiiiii.” Teresa Giudice and Witney Carson both described the photos as “beautiful.”

Pashkov himself also commented on the photos of Karagach by herself, writing simply, “Perfection.” The couple has been married since 2014. They announced they were expecting a baby together last November.
