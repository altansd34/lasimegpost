Nobody was expecting Keith Apicary to be a superstar dancer on the America’s Got Talent stage. He came off as super shy with a lack of professional training in his interview before his audition. But when he got out onto the stage, he literally shocked the entire world with his dance moves.

Keith Apicary Performs Completely Unexpected Dance Routine on ‘America’s Got Talent’

When Keith walked out onto the America’s Got Talent stage, the judges were very curious to see what he was going to do. The only problem was, Keith would not stop talking. The judges insisted for him to just start dancing. The music started and Keith stood awkwardly on the stage.

Everything changed when he launched into an energetic routine that had everyone completely mindblown. Keith’s moves ranged from twerking, doing the worm, and slowly air-humping the floor. Nobody expected him to have moves like this. He also threw in a hint of breakdancing.

Just when everything was going better than expected, Keith fell right off the stage. Everyone was concerned for him and peered over the judging panel to see if he was OK. Keith stood up had a smile plastered on his face. The judges all gave him a standing ovation and bowed down to him after the performance.

Simon Cowell admitted that at first he thought the routine was going to be “just awful” but then he “loved every second of it.” Cowell even said that he would rather watch Keith perform than go to the ballet.

“It was so unexpected and that’s what I love about this show,” Sofia Vergara said and we completely agree.

Keith broke down in tears and was clearly very happy by the reception he got from the audience and the judges. All of the judges voted him through to the next round of America’s Got Talent.
