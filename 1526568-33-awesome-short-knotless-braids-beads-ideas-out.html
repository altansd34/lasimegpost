33 awesome short knotless braids with beads ideas to try out

Short knotless braids with beads are a popular protective hairstyle that has taken the beauty world by storm. They are a perfect option if you're looking to switch up your look or give your hair a break from harsh treatment and styling tools. Adding beads to knotless braids is a creative way to elevate the hairstyle and make it more stylish. Whether you prefer subtle and simple or bold and intricate, there are countless ways to incorporate beads into your knotless braids.
