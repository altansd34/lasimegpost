Answers:

7.

The correct answer is 4.

6.

The words are "bacon," "infinity," and "beauty." The word "bacon" is extra because both "infinity" and "beauty" end in "y."

5.

If you turn these figures around, they all look like the given example. But the figure under letter C doesn't have the red box.

4.

The next number is 23. The thing here is, we increase the sum of the previous numbers by 1.

  • 2 + 5 = 7; 7 + 1 = 8
  • 5 + 8 = 13; 13 + 1 = 14
  • 8 + 14 = 22; 22 + 1 = 23

3.
