All Celebrity Performances During 'The Voice' Season 24 Finale

Posted by Tandra Barner on Sunday, August 4, 2024

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Trending

Photo by: Trae Patton/NBC

Reading Time: 2 minutes

The Voice promised a spectacular lineup of guest performers for its season 24 Finale, and they definitely delivered. During the show’s final episode, several renowned singing stars graced the stage to impress, inspire, and entertain the crowd through voices.

All Celebrity Performances from The Voice Season 24 Finale

1. Earth, Wind & Fire

During the musical group’s number, they performed a medley of their biggest hits “September,” “Boogie Wonderland” and “Let’s Groove.” With their nostalgic act, the classic ‘70s band had proven that the spotlight hasn’t left them behind. On stage, Earth, Wind & Fire shone with their glimmering green and pink coats.

2. Tyla

While looking sultry on stage, Tyla fascinated the crowd with a medley of “Truth or Dare” and “Water.” Tyla set the fire on stage and made it wet at the same time with her alluring performance.

3. AJR

Amid the NBC competition series’ Finale, AJR gave a dramatic and animated medley of “Bang!” and “Yes I’m A Mess.” AJR’s performance was perhaps the most playful among all celebrity performances of the season.

4. Teddy Swims

In a chill yet fiery performance of “Lose Control,” Teddy Swims effortlessly impressed the audience with his stunning vocals. Clearly, his act was one of the most perplexing performances during Finale.

5. Dan + Shay

Season 25 coaches Dan + Shay graced the stage of The Voice for a performance of “Bigger House.” This has practically given people a glimpse of how amazing the could be as coaches the next season.

6. Keith Urban

Country crooner Keith Urban also took the time to make his powerful presence felt on stage during the show’s Finale. Singing “Blue Ain’t Your Color,” Urban sent chills alongside the ethereal vibe of the stage.
