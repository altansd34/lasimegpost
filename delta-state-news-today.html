Delta State, Nigeria

Delta State is located in the south-south region of Nigeria and is bordered by Edo, Anambra, Rivers, and Bayelsa States. The Bight of Benin also covers about 160 kilometres of the state’s western coastline.

Population

As of 2006, the population of Delta State was estimated to be over 5.6 million people.

Governors

Here is a list of administrators and governors of Delta State, Nigeria, which was formed on August 27, 1991, when the old Bendel State was split into Edo State and Delta State:

  • Luke Chijiuba Ochulor, Administrator, 28 August 1991 - January 1992
  • Felix Ibru, Governor, January 1992 - November 1993
  • Abdulkadir Shehu (acting), Administrator, 17 November 1993 - 10 December 1993
  • Bassey Asuquo, Administrator, 10 December 1993 - 26 September 1994
  • Ibrahim Kefas, Administrator, 26 September 1994 - 22 August 1996
  • John Dungs, Administrator, 22 August 1996 - 12 August 1998
  • Walter Feghabo, Administrator, 12 August 1998 - 29 May 1999
  • James Ibori, Governor, 29 May 1999 - 29 May 2007
  • Emmanuel Uduaghan, Governor, 29 May 2007 - 29 May 2015
  • Ifeanyi Okowa, Governor, 29 May 2015 - 2023
  • Sheriff Oborevwori - 2023 - Present

    • Postal Code

    The postal code for the NIPOST headquarters in Asaba, the capital city of Delta State, is 320001. However, each town within the state has its unique postal code.

    • Delta State University Abraka

    • Novena University Ogeme
    • Admiralty University of Nigeria Ibusa
    • Western Delta University Oghara
    • Edwin Clark University Kiagbodo
