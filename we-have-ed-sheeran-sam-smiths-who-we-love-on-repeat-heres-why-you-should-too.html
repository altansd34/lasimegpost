We Have Ed Sheeran, Sam Smith's &quot;Who We Love&quot; on Repeat, Here's Why You Should Too

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, August 9, 2024

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Photo by Jason Mendez/Getty Images, Photo by: Todd Owyoung/NBC via Getty Images

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Sam Smith and Ed Sheeran have finally collaborated on a song, and we can’t get enough of it. After various years of admiring one another abilities, and being absolute friend goals, the two decided to work together on a brand new song “Who We Love.” The song is finally out, and we cannot stop hitting repeat.

“Who We Love” is a “Beautiful Classic Love Song”

Former The Voice Mega Mentor, Sheeran and Smith just released their first collaboration song “Who We Love.” The seamless blending of their voices makes “Who We Love,” what you may anticipate from both artists, with an added surprise. We really needed it, and it’s giving fans of the singers or love songs in general, a great tune to listen to.

“It was beautiful. We’ve been friends for a long time and we’ve always wanted to work on a piece of music together. And this happened just so naturally,” Smith said. “I love the record so much and he is such a dear special person to me, and to share something like this, it’s the end of the album and everything feels so perfect about it. I love it.”

The two have performed together in the past, but “Who We Love,” is their first ever official collaboration on a track. The song is featured on Smith’s newest album Gloria and is the final track of the 13 song album. Smith’s new album Gloria has a star-studded cast of musicians in addition to Sheeran, including Canadian Jessie Reyez and Kim Petras.

Ed Sheeran Sings For Courteney Cox

Another close friend of Sheeran, is actress Courteney Cox whom he sang for in their most recent Instagram collaboration. The two are celebrating the anniversary of Cox’s homecare products brand Homecourt.

Sheeran is seen playing his acoustic guitar while sitting next to Cox on the grass with a spread of Homecourt goods in front of them in the endearing video that was shared to the actress’ personal Instagram account and her Homecourt account.

“Homecourt, one year, Courteney Cox got the product to make you clean all night,” Sheeran sang.

Sheeran is playing a joyous song that sounds like a cover of Bob Marley’s “One Love,” while Cox smiles with him in what looks to be her backyard. Celebrities like Jennifer Garner and Chelsea Handler gave their congratulatory messages to Cox on the comments section of the post.
