America’s Got Talent Season 14 finalist Benicio Bryant has released his new single “I Can’t Dance to This,” as well as a colorful new music video to go along with it. Benicio is fully embracing his new style, and the song is a fun summer bop.

Benicio Bryant Releases ‘I Can’t Dance to This’

Benicio spent last month hyping up his new single and teasing a music video to go along with it. The 17-year-old teased fans with a brief clip from the song and encouraged everyone to pre-save it before its release at the end of the month.

Contrary to the song’s title, it’s actually very possible to dance to it. In fact, that’s the whole point of the lyrics. Benicio sings about a DJ playing something new and everyone thinking they can’t dance to it. But he’s out to prove them wrong, singing, “Watch me.”

The song’s music video shows Benicio wearing a series of glamorous outfits while hanging around the house doing chores like ironing, vacuuming, and cleaning the swimming pool. Standout looks include a pink suit, a furry purple coat, and a colorful oversize cardigan.

Benicio’s Fans Are Loving the New Track

The comments section for Benicio’s new music video is full of fans praising the singer’s new song and visuals. One fan wrote, “Stupendous! So catchy and amazing vocals! And what outfits! Showing some really amazing style, Beni!”

Several fans also told Benicio that he should be modeling, as they loved how he wore the fashions in the video. The singer has definitely cultivated a unique style in the years since he appeared on AGT in 2019, even dyeing his hair blond at one point.

Earlier this year, Benicio responded to a question about his new look on TikTok, saying he’s “happy” with it. And it sounds like his fans are, too.
