Little Kid Makes Rihanna His Photographer for Pic with Grealish, Pulisic

Posted by Kelle Repass on Wednesday, August 21, 2024
  • In a viral video, a young football fan asked Rihanna to take a photo of him and footballers Jack Grealish, Christian Pulisic, and Yunus Musah
  • The child, donning an AC Milan jersey, apparently didn't know who the American pop star was
  • The "We Found Love" hitmaker was in New York City to watch Manchester City take on AC Milan in a pre-season friendly

A video of a child asking pop superstar Rihanna to take a photo with him with footballers Jack Grealish, Christian Pulisic, and Yunus Musah has emerged.

Grealish and Manchester City were in New York for their pre-season in New York City and ran into the American musician.

After Grealish, Pulisic, and Musah had taken their photo with the "Umbrella" singer, a little football fan emerged from the side asking to take a picture with the Man City star.

The fan, wearing an AC Milan shirt, casually asked Rihanna to take a photo of him and the footballers, leaving everyone in the room amazed.

The pop star, without hesitation, gladly took the phone, sat on a chair, and took a photo of the child before cheers rang around the room. The kid apparently didn't know who Rihanna was.

The world-famous musician was in the stands to watch Man City take on AC Milan in a friendly clash.

It was a wholesome moment and an unforgettable one for the child. The video shared by PUMA Football went viral on social media, and fans praised Rihanna for accepting the kid's request.
