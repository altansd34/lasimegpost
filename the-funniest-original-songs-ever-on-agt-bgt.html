We’ve seen some great original songs on both America’s Got Talent and Britain’s Got Talent, but here are some of the most unique. Here are some of the most funny original songs ever on AGT and BGT.

These singer/songwriters came to the stage to show off their vocal skills and ridiculous lyrics. Be ready to roll on the floor laughing along with these seven unique tunes.

1. Ben Lapidus

This America’s Got Talent singer won over the audience with his original parmesan cheese song. Even though the judges all gave Ben their red buzzers, the audience persuaded them into changing their mind. This song is ridiculous, but the audience absolutely loved it.

2. Jonny Awsum

This Britain’s Got Talent singer began his audition by asking hosts Ant and Dec to join him on stage. After teaching them a simple dance, Jonny began singing a song about being in a musical featuring Ant and Dec. Jonny was able to get the audience cheering for him after this hilarious tune.

3. Ray Jessel

This 84 year old took the America’s Got Talent stage to sing a song and play the piano. The song was completely normal, until the singer explained that the person they were singing about actually has a penis. The judges were dying of laughter during this audition.

4. Lewberger

On America’s Got Talent season 16, three men took the stage to sing a song about Terry Crews. The performance quickly turned into a strip tease, involving the show’s host.

5. Johnny Showcase

Johnny Showcase took the America’s Got Talent stage with his background singers/dancers. The group’s silly song was about getting sensual instead of sexual because he’s married. The audience absolutely loved this group.

6. Ryan Beard

This 18 year old took the America’s Got Talent stage in season 11 to sing his song “Ladies Man.” After explaining that he was homeschooled and never had a girlfriend, he belted out the comedic tune.

7. Robert White

Robert took the Britain’s Got Talent stage to roast the judges with his song. The song was about working at a computer store, but mixed each judge into the narrative. The audience thought his song was absolutely hilarious.
