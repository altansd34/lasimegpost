The Real Simon Cowell Won't Be Talking During The 'AGT' Season 18 Auditions

Posted by Billy Koelling on Thursday, August 29, 2024

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent America's Got Talent

WireImage,

Reading Time: 2 minutes

The filming of America’s Got Talent Season 18 took an unexpected turn when Simon Cowell, one of the show’s judges, lost his voice early on in production. The music executive and reality TV personality was left speechless, prompting his fellow judges to take on the task of giving his reactions for the show’s auditions.

Simon Cowell Lost His Voice While Filming AGT

Cowell is famous for his sharp tongue and no-holds-barred criticisms as a judge on America’s Got Talent. During the filming of the show’s season 18, Cowell lost his voice, which led his fellow judges to take on his role temporarily.

According to sources, Cowell’s loss of voice came as a result of talking too much during filming. His fellow judges, Sofia Vergara, Howie Mandel, and Heidi Klum, found humor in the situation and stepped in to fill the void left by Cowell’s silence.

Despite his voicelessness, Cowell managed to make light of the situation, quipping in a hoarse whisper that he was “at a loss for words.” The unexpected turn of events created a unique dynamic among the judges, with Mandel joking that it was “serendipitous” that Cowell lost his voice during the most exciting season yet.

Vergara even joked that Cowell “nominated” her to give his reactions, adding that they were passing notes like they were back in school. This unexpected twist in the show’s production created a humorous dynamic among the judges and added a unique element to the season’s auditions.

Fans Are Concerned Over The Judges’ Weight Loss

Cowell’s recent weight loss has been a topic of concern among fans. Reportedly, Cowell has lost an estimated 60 lbs and photographers have captured him looking thinner than ever during filming auditions of AGT and Britain’s Got Talent in Manchester and London.

Fans have taken to social media to express their concerns about Cowell’s weight loss. With some commenting on his thin frame and others suggesting that he has undergone cosmetic treatments.

Cowell stated that he achieved his weight loss by reevaluating his diet and reducing his meat intake, as well as cutting out sugar, dairy, and gluten from his daily intake. Despite fans’ concerns, Cowell has stated that he is feeling healthy and happy with his new physique.
