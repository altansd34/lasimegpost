A Baby Born Without a Nose Was Called Voldemort, and She Is Not Afraid to Share Her Uniqueness

Posted by Patria Henriques on Saturday, August 24, 2024

This little girl, born without a nose and nicknamed “Voldemort,” possesses physical features that set her apart from the average person. Her remarkable story challenges us to embrace the beauty and diversity that exists within the human form, redefining our understanding of what it means to be truly extraordinary.

She was born with a rare condition.

Tessa was born with a rare condition called complete congenital arhinia, which means she was born without a nose. This anomaly, affecting small numbers of people in the world, presented unique challenges for Tessa from the very beginning of her life. However, she has refused to let her condition define her and has embraced life with unmatched enthusiasm.

Arhinia occurs when the nose fails to develop in the womb, resulting in the absence of this facial feature. In Tessa’s case, her condition also means that she does not have the olfactory system, the part of the brain responsible for recognizing smells. Despite the challenges she faced, Tessa’s parents saw her as a beautiful and extraordinary girl.

When Tessa’s parents, Grainne and Nathan Evans, discovered their unborn child’s facial deformity during an ultrasound, they were faced with a wave of emotions. Five months into the pregnancy, they learned that their daughter would be born without a nose. Despite the initial shock, they resolved to provide Tessa with the best possible care and support.

Tessa’s journey has been marked by numerous medical procedures and surgeries.

Shortly after her birth, she was transferred to the intensive care unit, where she underwent a tracheotomy to facilitate breathing. At just eleven months old, Tessa had to undergo cataract surgery, further adding to the challenges she faced at such a tender age.

Two years later, Tessa underwent cosmetic surgery to have prostheses implanted beneath her skin, laying the groundwork for the construction of an artificial nose in the future. The decision to proceed with the surgery was not taken lightly by her parents, but they saw it as an opportunity to gradually enhance Tessa’s appearance over time, making her profile more “normal.” This involved using bone and skin grafts, a process typically recommended during adolescence when the face has finished growing.

Despite these physical hardships, Tessa’s spirit remains unbroken.

Tessa has learned to adapt to a life without a nose, breathing through her mouth and being deprived of the sense of smell that most of us take for granted. Tessa’s infectious joy and zest for life serve as a testament to her unwavering determination to live to the fullest.

Her story has garnered attention from around the world, inspiring countless individuals who face their own challenges. Her strength and resilience remind us that true beauty lies not in physical appearance but in the indomitable spirit that resides within each of us. Tessa’s journey serves as a beacon of hope, reminding us that we can overcome any obstacle with determination, courage, and the support of loved ones.

In a world where it’s easy to be consumed by negativity, stories like Tessa’s shine a light on the triumph of the human spirit. Tessa’s journey is far from over, and her story continues to inspire countless individuals around the world.
