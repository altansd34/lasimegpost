'American Idol' Winner Carrie Underwood Set To Perform Live On The 'Today' Show

Posted by Billy Koelling on Saturday, August 24, 2024

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Photo by Emma McIntyre/Getty Images for SiriusXM

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Renowned country music sensation and winner of the fourth season of American Idol, Carrie Underwood is all set to grace the TODAY Plaza in New York City on Thursday, September 14, as part of the Citi Concert Series on TODAY. Her live performance promises to be a highlight of the morning, featuring some of her latest and greatest hits.

Carrie Underwood Will be Performing Live on The Today Show

Underwood is a household name in the world of country music and has captured the hearts of fans worldwide with her powerhouse vocals and compelling storytelling. Her chart-topping hits, including “Before He Cheats,” “Jesus, Take the Wheel,” “Blown Away,” and “Something in the Water,” have not only dominated the country music charts but have also garnered crossover success on the mainstream pop charts.

As fans eagerly anticipate her spectacular live performance on the TODAY Plaza, they can look forward to experiencing the magic of these iconic songs and more. The date and time for this musical extravaganza are Thursday, September 14, with check-in beginning at 5:15 a.m.

The venue for this electrifying event is the iconic Rockefeller Plaza, situated at 35 West 48th Street, between Fifth and Sixth Avenues, in the heart of New York City. To ensure a smooth entry, remember to bring a valid photo ID for each individual in your party.

If attending in person isn’t feasible, you can still savor Underwood’s captivating performance online, and it won’t cost you a dime. Tune in to the TODAY show, airing on your local NBC station, starting at 7 a.m. on the day of the concert, and immerse yourself in the magic of her live music.

Live Viewers Can Secure a Fan Pass

For those aiming to secure a Fan Pass and experience the event in person, here are the key details. Register now for a chance to receive a Fan Pass, which allows you to bring along three guests, making it a total of four attendees in your party.

To ensure a smooth and enjoyable experience, Fan Pass recipients and General Admission attendees should arrive by 5 a.m. Please note that for security reasons, a concert line cannot form until 7 p.m. the day before the performance.

Fan Pass notifications will be sent via email approximately one week prior to the concert date. However, if you do not receive a Fan Pass, don’t worry; you can still join the General Admission line on the morning of the concert, and you’ll be admitted if space permits. Regardless of the weather conditions, the show typically goes on as scheduled, rain or shine.
