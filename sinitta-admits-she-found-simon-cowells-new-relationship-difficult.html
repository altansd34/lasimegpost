Sinitta revealed all in a recent interview on The Shapes of Stories Podcast. She said that she took it ‘quite hard’ when Simon Cowell began dating again.

Sinitta: Simon Cowell Finding New Love Was ‘Difficult’

Sinitta is known for telling it how it is, and she didn’t hold back on her recent appearance on “The Shapes of Stories” podcast.

RELATED: SIMON COWELL SHOWS OFF MAJOR WEIGHT LOSS ARRIVING AT ‘AGT’

Speaking on her relationship with Simon Cowell and her time on The X Factor, Sinitta was asked how she felt after Cowell found new love.

“I did find it difficult at first,” Sinitta admitted. “I’d sort of loved Simon my whole life and a big part of me thought that once we got older and more settled, that we would end up together, get married and be together because he’d always been in my life.”

The two dated on and off after meeting in the 1980s before Cowell began dating his current partner, Lauren Silverman. Sinitta did mention in the interview that she is now on good terms with Cowell. She also revealed she is godmother to Cowell’s son, Eric.

Simon Cowell Returns to AGT, Keeps ‘BGT’ On Hold

The rumors are true. Cowell will return to America’s Got Talent for a much anticipated season of the show alongside Sofia Vergara, Howie Mandel, and Heidi Klum. The new season is set to air June 1.

RELATED: SIMON COWELL’S POSSIBLE PLANS FOR SHARON OSBOURNE AND PIERS MORGAN

After the announcement that Britain’s Got Talent won’t air this year due to strict COVID-19 restrictions in the United Kingdom, Cowell was keen to air at least one of the shows he appears on.

Filming is underway on NBC’s hit talent show. Auditions are taking place at the Pasadena Civic Auditorium in Los Angeles. While the details around how filming will work this season aren’t completely clear, we can expect the show to follow in the steps of other talent shows that filmed during the pandemic. Either no audience or a smaller, socially distanced crowd will likely join the judges on their search for America’s next winner.
