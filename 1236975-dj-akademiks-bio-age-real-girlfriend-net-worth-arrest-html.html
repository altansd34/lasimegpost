Academic and career

He went on to graduate from high school, and attend Rutgers University in New Jersey where he graduated with a bachelor’s degree in Biomathematics. He began as a producer in college but recorded very little success at it hence switching to becoming a DJ. He played at different parties, barbeques, as well as engagements in college. He began to upload videos of his music and play for the Rutgers University radio station on Saturday evenings from 6 pm to 8 pm, something that he did until 2013.

He discovered the power of the internet when he started getting strange calls from people inviting him to play at their parties in the outskirts of New Jersey. Due to this, he came up with a website called Late Night Creeps. Later, he chose to move from videos posts of himself at parties to videos of him giving his views on different subjects concerning rap music and entertainment.
