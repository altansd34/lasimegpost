Mercy Chinwo Marks Wedding Anniversary With Loved-up Pics: It's Been 2yrs of God's Faithfulness&quot;

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, August 15, 2024
  • Gospel singer Mercy Chinwe has celebrated her wedding anniversary with a love note and loved up pictures
  • In the post she shared, she was wearing a matching cloth with her husband which was complimented with matching caps by the two of them
  • The singer said that it was two years of God's faithfulness and mercy, she prayed for her marriage

Nigerian gospel singer, Mercy Chinwo, has gushed over her marriage of two years. The singer, who welcomed a son last year, shared loved-up pictures with her husband.

Accompanying it with a short love note, the mother of one announced that it was two years of God's faithfulness and mercy over her marriage.

She also wished herself and her husband many more years of togetherness.
