'AGT' Alum Marcelito Pomoy Meets Up With Roland Abante For a Concert

Posted by Kelle Repass on Sunday, August 18, 2024

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent America's Got Talent

Photo by JC Olivera/FilmMagic, Photo by Steve Granitz/FilmMagic

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Fans of America’s Got Talent were recently treated to an unexpected surprise. Filipino singing sensations Marcelito Pomoy, and Roland Abante were seen together at the airport. The excitement is through the roof as fans eagerly await a possible performance by the two artists.

Marcelito Pomoy, Roland Abante Are Possibly Performing Together at a Concert

The Instagram post featuring Pomoy and Abante at the airport comes as a surprise to fans of both artists. This moment is one of camaraderie and mutual respect between two talented Filipino musicians.

Pomoy first gained fame in 2011 when he emerged as the winner of Pilipinas Got Talent season two. However, his musical odyssey didn’t end there. In 2020, he mesmerized the international stage on AGT: The Champions Season Two, where he earned a well-deserved fourth-place finish.

On the other hand, Abante had his own share of AGT Season 18 glory. He made a significant impact on the show with his soulful renditions of classic songs. His unforgettable rendition of “When a Man Loves a Woman” earned him a standing ovation from the judges. It quickly garnered millions of views on YouTube.

The most exciting part of this announcement is the upcoming birthday concert, which promises to be a memorable musical extravaganza. Pomoy and Abante’s collaboration on this special occasion is a treat for music lovers, and it’s bound to be a night filled with soul-stirring performances and unforgettable moments.

As they prepare to take the stage in Burbank, Los Angeles, on September 22, fans eagerly await the magic that Pomoy and Abante will undoubtedly create together.

Roland Abante Recently Exited AGT

While the anticipation for Pomoy’s birthday concert with Abante continues to build, fans are also reflecting on Abante’s recent journey on AGT Season 18. The Filipino artist, known affectionately as “Bunot,” left an incredible mark on the show, earning two standing ovations from the judges during his time in the competition.

Abante’s initial audition, where he delivered a soulful rendition of “When a Man Loves a Woman,” left judges and viewers in awe. His powerful voice and heartfelt performance made an immediate connection with the audience. Although he didn’t receive the coveted Golden Buzzer, he earned immense praise from judges Simon Cowell, Sofia Vergara, Heidi Klum, and Howie Mandel.

In his subsequent performance, Abante chose to tackle Whitney Houston’s iconic ballad, “I Will Always Love You.” Once again, his performance left the judges and the audience moved by his passion and talent. Abante eventually exited the qualifying round of AGT, but audiences will remember his journey on the show for the musical prowess he showcased and the impact of his soul-stirring performances.
