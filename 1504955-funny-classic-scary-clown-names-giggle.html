How do you choose a clown name?

When playing clown games with children, allow them to list names of careers they desire, their favorite animals, cities, food, films, etc. Write the names on slips of paper and place them in a bowl. Pick any piece randomly and use the name written on it.

What are the 4 main types of clowns?

The three common types of clowns that appear in the circus are the Auguste, the whiteface, and the character. The fourth type, the tramp or hobo clown, is similar to the other three types but is often recognized separately.

What is the name of the famous sad clown?

Emmett Leo Kelly (born in 1898 — died in 1979) was one of America's greatest circus clowns. He was best known for his role as Weary Willie — a sad tramp with a beard, a bulbous nose, and dressed in tattered clothes.

Who was the saddest clown in history?

Emmett Kelly (Emmett Leo Kelly) is the saddest clown in history. Instead of wearing cheerful, bright clothes and having a grin-painted face, he wore dark-colored rags, and his face was forever sad.

What was the first evil clown?

British star Joseph 'Joe' Grimaldi was the greatest clown of the 19th Century and the first evil clown in history. His alter-ego character, Joey, was known for terrorizing people worldwide. He wore a white face and had exaggerated make-up and movements.

Who is the father of clowns?

Joseph 'Joe' Grimaldi is the founding father of modern-day clowns. His clowning style originated in the Italian 'commedia dell'arte' of the 16th Century.
