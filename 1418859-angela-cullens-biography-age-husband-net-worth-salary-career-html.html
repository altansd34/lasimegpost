Angela Cullen's biography

Cullen was born in Devonport, a small bayside village on the coast of Auckland, New Zealand. She attended Auckland University of Technology and graduated with a Bachelor's degree in health science, majoring in physiotherapy.

How old is Angela Cullen?

Occupation

Angela is a sportswoman and a physiotherapist. She is known for being Lewis Hamilton's personal trainer. She became passionate about sports from a young age and participated in various games such as swimming, basketball, hockey, and cricket.

After graduating, Lewis Hamilton's trainer moved to England and started working as a senior physiotherapist for London's English Institute of Sports. Sher worked there between the years 2000 and 2006 and played a significant role in preparing the British Olympic team.

Cullen went back to New Zealand in 2006. She worked with SPARC High-Performance NZ as a senior advisor of performance services until 2010. She has also worked as the director of the Body Performance Sports Injury Clinic.
