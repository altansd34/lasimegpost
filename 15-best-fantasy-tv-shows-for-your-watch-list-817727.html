15+ Best Fantasy TV Shows for Your Watch List

Posted by Tandra Barner on Wednesday, August 21, 2024

Life can get pretty routine sometimes. We know what to expect, and it can feel like we’re stuck in a loop. That’s when diving into the world of fantasy can be a real game-changer. With today’s amazing technology and special effects, what we see on TV feels more real than ever.

Whether you’re a die-hard fantasy fan or just curious, these shows will whisk you away to exciting new places. So grab some popcorn and get ready for an adventure like no other with the best fantasy TV shows!

1. The Witcher

This tale follows the exploits of Geralt of Rivia, a monster hunter known as the Witcher, as he navigates a world full of magic, beasts, and political intrigue. Geralt explores the world, accepting different contracts to hunt hazardous monsters and becoming engaged in greater wars between kingdoms and supernatural beings.

While searching for his own destiny, he makes strange allies and comes across strong sorceresses. Through action-packed escapades and moral quandaries, Geralt discovers the subtleties of good and evil, loyalty, and fate.

You can find this TV show on Netflix.
IMDb Rating: ⭐ 8.0/10

2. Buffy the Vampire Slayer

This is the story of Buffy Summers, a young woman chosen to battle vampires, demons, and other supernatural forces. Guided by her Watcher, Rupert Giles, Buffy balances her responsibilities as the Slayer with her life as a high school student in Sunnydale, a town located atop a Hellmouth, a gateway to demonic dimensions.

With her loyal friends Willow and Xander by her side, Buffy faces off against various villains, including vampires like Angel and Spike, as well as formidable entities such as the Master and the Mayor. Throughout the series, Buffy struggles with her destiny, relationships, and personal demons, while also forging alliances with other Slayers and mystical beings.

You can find this TV show on Disney+.
IMDb Rating: ⭐ 8.3/10

3. Game of Thrones

This gripping tale is set in the fictional continents of Westeros and Essos. The series revolves around the power struggles among noble families vying for control of the Iron Throne and the Seven Kingdoms of Westeros. Central characters include members of noble houses such as the Starks, Lannisters, and Targaryens, each with their own ambitions, strengths, and flaws.

As political intrigue and warfare escalate, characters must navigate treacherous terrain, forging alliances, making sacrifices, and facing moral dilemmas to survive in a world where power is the ultimate currency. Amidst the power struggles, mystical elements, including dragons, magic, and prophecies, play a significant role, adding layers of complexity to the narrative.

With each season, Game of Thrones delivers shocking plot twists, epic battles, and intricate character development, captivating audiences with its exploration of themes such as loyalty, betrayal, honor, and the consequences of ambition.

You can find this TV show on HBO Max.
IMDb Rating: ⭐ 9.2/10

4. Doctor Who

Doctor Who follows the adventures of the Doctor, a Time Lord from the planet Gallifrey who travels through time and space in the TARDIS, a time machine disguised as a British police box. Alongside various companions, the Doctor combats evil and injustice across the universe, facing off against a wide array of villains and monsters.

Throughout the series, the Doctor’s travels take them to different planets, historical periods, and alternate dimensions, encountering historical figures, extraterrestrial civilizations, and enigmatic beings known as the Time Lords. Throughout their adventures, the Doctor’s companions undergo personal growth and development, while the Doctor themselves confront their own morality and the consequences of their actions.

You can find this TV show on Apple TV+.
IMDb Rating: ⭐ 8.4/10

5. The Shannara Chronicles

This is the story of Wil Ohmsford, Amberle Elessedil, and Eretria as they embark on a quest to save the Four Lands from an ancient evil known as the Dagda Mor. Guided by the druid Allanon, they must retrieve the lost Elfstones, powerful artifacts capable of sealing the demon away for eternity. As they venture through treacherous landscapes and face formidable foes, including the ruthless Warlock Lord, the trio discovers hidden truths about their own destinies and the fate of their world.

As they travel, they come across magical creatures, face political intrigue, and confront personal challenges. With time running out and the fate of the Four Lands at stake, Wil, Amberle, and Eretria must set aside their differences and accept their roles as unlikely heroes to stand against the encroaching darkness threatening their world.

You can find this TV show on Amazon Prime Video.
IMDb Rating: ⭐ 7.1/10

6. Stranger Things

The plot line revolves around a group of kids in a small town who encounter supernatural mysteries and government conspiracies. When one of their friends goes missing, they embark on a quest to find him and uncover the truth behind mysterious occurrences in their town.

During their travels, they meet a mysterious girl with psychokinetic abilities who assists them in their quest. Through their adventures, they learn about bravery and the importance of standing up to evil forces. This is surely one of the best fantasy TV shows to add to your watch list.

You can find this TV show on Netflix.
IMDb Rating: ⭐ 8.7/10

7. Wednesday

Wednesday follows the adventures of the iconic character Wednesday Addams from The Addams Family as she navigates the challenges of growing up and attending Nevermore Academy. Alongside her peculiar classmates, she uncovers dark secrets about her family’s past while using her unique abilities to solve mysteries and protect her friends.

Throughout her experiences, she discovers how to come to terms with the events around her, the evolving meaning of companionship, and acknowledge her own inner struggles in a world that frequently misinterprets her.

You can find this TV show on Netflix.
IMDb Rating: ⭐ 8.1/10

8. The Mandalorian

The plot in this show follows the journey of a lone bounty hunter as he travels across the galaxy, completing missions and encountering various characters along the way. Set in the Star Wars universe, the series explores themes of honor, loyalty, and redemption as the Mandalorian forms unexpected connections and faces moral dilemmas.

Through action-packed adventures and heartwarming moments, he learns about the true meaning of belonging and protecting those he cares about.

You can find this TV show on Disney+.
IMDb Rating: ⭐ 8.7/10

9. Charmed

Charmed introduces us to three sisters: Prue, Piper, and Phoebe. Their lives take an astonishing turn when they discover they are the Charmed Ones, endowed with powerful witchcraft talents. Together, they use their power to combat evil forces and protect the innocent. Their trip is plagued with otherworldly encounters, tumultuous personal relationships, and discoveries about their family’s history.

Encountering these obstacles, they realize the profound significance of sisterhood, bravery, and responsibly using their powers for the greater good.

You can find this TV show on Peacock.
IMDb Rating: ⭐ 7.2/10

10. The Originals

In The Originals, we delve into the lives of the Mikaelson siblings, the very first vampires and among the most formidable creatures in existence. Their story begins with their return to New Orleans, the city they once played a significant role in shaping centuries ago. Upon their arrival, they find themselves entangled in a power struggle with different supernatural factions vying for control.

As they deal with complex alliances and confront old enemies, they also face their own family issues and personal grudges. Through their challenges, the Mikaelsons learn about making amends, the sacrifices they must make, and the importance of family.

You can find this TV show on HBO Max.
IMDb Rating: ⭐ 8.3/10

11. H2O: Just Add Water

In H2O: Just Add Water, we meet three teenage girls whose lives change forever when they inadvertently become mermaids after a transformative encounter with water from a mysterious moon pool. Cleo, Emma, and Rikki struggle to balance their newfound aquatic abilities with the challenges of typical teenage life.

As they manage the difficulties of keeping their secret, they meet other mermaids, face dangers in the water, and discover the importance of supporting each other and using their strengths.

You can find this TV show on Netflix.
IMDb Rating: ⭐ 7.2/10

12. Manifest

Manifest tells the story of the passengers aboard Flight 828, who inexplicably jump forward five years in time during what should have been a routine flight. When they land, they’re shocked to learn that everyone thought to be on board is presumed dead. As they try to adjust to their new reality, they face the challenge of fitting back into their old lives while also uncovering the truth behind their disappearance.

Through their adventures, they stumble upon strange events and realize they might have developed supernatural powers. As they deal with friendships, moral dilemmas, and the big mystery of their disappearance, they dive deeper into the puzzle that surrounds them.

You can find this TV show on Netflix.
IMDb Rating: ⭐ 7.1/10

13. Supernatural

Supernatural centers around the Winchester brothers, Sam and Dean, who dedicate their lives to hunting down supernatural creatures such as ghosts, demons, and monsters. Embarking on a relentless quest, they travel across the country investigating paranormal phenomena and facing off against formidable evil forces.

Their mission is fueled by a desire to avenge their mother’s tragic death and uncover the truth behind their father’s disappearance. With unwavering determination, Sam and Dean vow to protect innocent lives from the ever-present threats lurking in the shadows.

You can find this TV show on HBO Max.
IMDb Rating: ⭐ 8.4/10

14. The 100

In the aftermath of a nuclear apocalypse, The 100 thrusts a group of juvenile delinquents from the space station called the Ark back to Earth. Their mission: to determine if the planet is habitable once more. However, upon their return, they face a harsh reality filled with mutated creatures and rival factions of survivors.

Amidst the challenges of the unforgiving wilderness, they navigate internal conflicts and moral dilemmas, grappling with their newfound freedom and responsibilities. As they adapt to their surroundings, they learn the complexities of human nature.

You can find this TV show on Netflix.
IMDb Rating: ⭐ 7.6/10

15. Shadowhunters

In Shadowhunters, we’re introduced to Clary Fray, an ordinary teenager whose world is turned upside down when she discovers her true lineage as a Shadowhunter—a human-angel hybrid dedicated to hunting demons. When her mother disappears under mysterious circumstances, Clary teams up with fellow Shadowhunters like Jace Wayland and Alec Lightwood to track her down and unearth the secrets of her heritage.

As they journey together, facing off against demons and encountering magical beings, they also navigate the complexities of their relationships with each other. Through their shared trials and triumphs, Clary discovers the truth about her past, the significance of loyalty and friendship, and the eternal struggle between good and evil.

You can find this TV show on Netflix.
IMDb Rating: ⭐ 6.5/10

16. Teen Wolf

Teen Wolf follows the life of high school student Scott McCall, who gets bitten by a werewolf and gains supernatural abilities. He struggles to balance his newfound powers with his everyday life while keeping his secret from his friends and family. As Scott navigates the challenges of being a teenager, he encounters other supernatural creatures and battles against them to protect his loved ones and his town.

Through his journey, he learns about courage, loyalty, and the true meaning of being a hero. Being part of one of the best fantasy TV shows, this series had a rather unexplainable ending for many of the fans, which resulted in a final movie that was made years after the closing episode of the show, so make sure to check that one too after watching it.

You can find this TV show on Amazon Prime Video.
IMDb Rating: ⭐ 7.7/10

17. Once Upon a Time

This show is about the residents of the town of Storybrooke, where fairy tale characters are cursed to live without knowledge of their true identities. Emma Swan, the daughter of Snow White and Prince Charming, arrives in Storybrooke and learns of the curse from her son, Henry. Determined to break the curse and restore everyone’s memories, Emma must confront the powerful Mayor Regina Mills, also known as the Evil Queen.

You can find this TV show on Disney+.
IMDb Rating: ⭐ 7.7/10

18. House of the Dragon

House of the Dragon is a prequel to Game of Thrones and follows the rise of House Targaryen, a noble family known for their dragons. Set centuries before the events of Game of Thrones, the series explores the Targaryen civil war known as the Dance of the Dragons.

As rival factions within the family vie for power and control of the Iron Throne, viewers witness epic battles, political intrigue, and the fiery might of dragons. Amidst the chaos, alliances are forged and broken, secrets are uncovered, and the fate of Westeros hangs in the balance.

You can find this TV show on HBO Max.
IMDb Rating: ⭐ 8.4/10
